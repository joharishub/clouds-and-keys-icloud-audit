🔑 Account Protection

Why it matters: Keeps your sign-in and recovery info current so you don’t lose access or miss alerts.

Action: Settings → [your name] → Sign-In & Security → Verify Emails & Phone Numbers.

Why it matters: Blocks password-only logins and ensures only your devices receive verification codes.

Action: Settings → [your name] → Sign-In & Security → Two-Factor Authentication → ON + Verify Trusted Phone Number and devices.

Why it matters: Master key to regain access (essential with Advanced Data Protection).

Action: Settings → [your name] → Sign-In & Security → Recovery Key → On / Create New, if the key has been lost or stolen.

Why it matters: End-to-end encrypts most iCloud data so only you can access it.

Action: Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Advanced Data Protection → On.

Why it matters: Phishing-resistant login with no fallbacks; dramatically reduces account-takeover risk.

Action: Settings → [your name] → Sign-In & Security → Two-Factor Authentication → Security Keys → Add Security Keys.

Why it matters: Reduces attack surface from browser-based access and phishing.

Action: Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Access iCloud Data on the Web → Off (enable only when needed).

Why it matters: Trusted people can help you recover your account if you’re locked out.

Action: Settings → [your name] → Sign-In & Security → Recovery Methods → Recovery Contacts → Add Recovery Contact.

Why it matters: Stores and syncs strong, unique passwords and passkeys securely across devices.

Action: Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Passwords → Sync this iPhone → On.

📱 Device Protection

Why it matters: Core of iPhone encryption - without a passcode, anyone with your phone can read your data.

Action: Settings → Face ID & Passcode → Require Passcode → Immediately (use 6+ digits).

Why it matters: Ensures you can restore your data after loss, theft, or damage.

Action: Settings → [your name] → iCloud → iCloud Backup → Back Up This iPhone → On.

Why it matters: Lets you locate and lock or wipe a lost/stolen device so it can’t be reused.

Action: Settings → [your name] → Find My → Find My iPhone → Turn on Find My iPhone, Find My network, and Send Last Location.

Why it matters: Stops brute-force passcode guessing by wiping data after too many failures.

Action: Settings → Face ID & Passcode → Erase Data → On.

Why it matters: Installs the latest security patches quickly to close known vulnerabilities.

Action: Settings → General → Software Update → Automatic Updates → “Automatically Install.”

Why it matters: Blocks sensitive account changes even if a thief knows your passcode.

Action: Settings → Face ID & Passcode → Stolen Device Protection → On.

🌐 Privacy

Why it matters: Quickly reset or review sharing and access in emergencies or after risk events.

Action: Settings → Privacy & Security → Safety Check → Manage Sharing & Access (or Emergency Reset when urgent).

Why it matters: Prevents unlock while you’re asleep or not looking at the device.

Action: Settings → Face ID & Passcode → Require Attention for Face ID → On.

Why it matters: Confirms secure iMessage/FaceTime sessions with trusted contacts.

Action: Settings → [your name] → Contact Key Verification → On.
